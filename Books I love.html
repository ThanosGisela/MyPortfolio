Books I Love!



Spy School

Spy School is a serier for middle grade written by the author Stuart Gibbs. It is about a boy (Ben Ripley) who is recruited
to a top secret espionage training academy. Despite Ben's special gift for math, he had been brought to the academy as a
patsy. This immediately had Ben in trouble's way. With devious plots, astounding betrayals, and amazing descriptions, Spy School
is easily one of my favorite series.
